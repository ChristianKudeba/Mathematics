5. **Three-step proof.**

    **Step 1 (Reduction to Siegel set + Weil-rep formula).** Used left $SL_2(F)$-automorphy to reduce $g \to g \in \mathfrak{S}$; Weil-rep formulas at $n_0 m(a) k$ yield
    $$|(\omega_\psi(g)\phi)(y)| = |a|_{\mathbb{A}_F} \cdot |\phi^k(ay)|,$$
    with $\phi^k = \omega_\psi(k)\phi$. By Fréchet continuity of the Weil rep (YZZ §3.1.2) + compactness of $K$, the orbit $\{\omega_\psi(k)\phi : k \in K\}$ is Fréchet-compact, hence uniformly bounded in every Schwartz seminorm. The pointwise majorant $\Phi(y) := \sup_k |\omega_\psi(k)\phi(y)|$ has rapid decay $\Phi(y) \leq C_M(1+\|y\|)^{-M}$ for every $M$.

    **Step 2 (Lattice-sum reduction).** $|\Theta(g, h; \phi)| \leq |a|_{\mathbb{A}_F} \cdot S(a, h)$ with $S(a, h) := \sum_{x \in E} \Phi(a h^{-1} x)$.

    **Step 3 (Lattice-Gaussian estimate).**
    - **(3α)** Reduce $h$ by $O(V_2)(F)$-automorphy (Prop II.1.b part 3) to a compact fundamental set $\mathfrak{F} \subset O(V_2)(\mathbb{A}_F)$ for $[O(V_2)]$. Compactness from $V_2$ anisotropic.
    - **(3β)** Finite spinor class number: $\mathfrak{F}_f$ is covered by finitely many cosets $h_f^{(j)} K^{O(V_2)}_f$ with $K^{O(V_2)}_f = O(V_2)(\widehat{\mathcal{O}}_F)$. Right $K^{O(V_2)}_f$-action preserves $\widehat{\mathcal{O}}_E$, so $S(a, h)$ depends on $h_f$ only through the finite list $\{h_f^{(1)}, \ldots, h_f^{(J)}\}$. Yields uniform constant $C_h := \max_j \mathrm{covol}(L^{(j)})$. (Cites Platonov–Rapinchuk "Algebraic Groups and Number Theory" §5.1 for finite spinor class number of an anisotropic torus.)
    - **Lattice covolume:** $\mathrm{covol}(L(a_f, h^{(j)}_f)) \asymp_\Phi C_h \cdot |a_f|_{\mathbb{A}_{F,f}}$ uniformly.

Then $|a_\infty|_\infty \geq c_0$, archimedean lattice spacing of $a_\infty \mathcal{O}_E$ shrinks to $\asymp |a_\infty|_\infty^{1/2}$ (since $|a_\infty|_\infty = a_\infty\bar a_\infty$ is the $\mathbb{C}$-norm-squared); Gaussian characteristic length-scale $|a_\infty|_\infty^{-1/2}$ vs lattice spacing $|a_\infty|_\infty^{1/2}$ → only $y = 0$ (plus small-norm tail) contributes; $S(a, h) \ll 1$.
    - **Combine:** $|\Theta(g, h; \phi)| \leq |a|_{\mathbb{A}_F} \cdot S \ll H(g)$, so $N(\phi) = 1$ suffices.
