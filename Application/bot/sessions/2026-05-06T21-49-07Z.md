## Result

**Structural decomposition (rigorous integer identity):**
$$T_<(N) = T_{\rm half}(N) + A(N) + B(N), \qquad T_>(N) = T_{\rm half}(N) - A(N) - B(N).$$

**Closed form for $A^\infty$ (rigorous via SelbergŌĆōDelange):**
$$A(N) = \sum_{e \,\mathrm{sf}, e \le N} \rho(e) = R H(1) \cdot N + O_A(N (\log N)^{-A}).$$

Verified empirically: at $N = 10^6$, $A(N) - R H(1) N = +0.5$ (absolute).

**Reduction of $c_0^T$ to a single empirical quantity:**
$$c_0^T = 2(R H'(1) + \gamma_K H(1) - R H(1)) - 2 B^\infty = 1.158730 - 2 B^\infty,$$
*conditional on existence of* $B^\infty := \lim_{N \to \infty} B(N)/N$.

**Empirical** $B^\infty \approx 0.086 \pm 0.002$ at $N \in [10^4, 10^6]$;
hence $c_0^T \approx 0.987$ matching the prev-session empirical $0.984 \pm 0.005$.
